Room

Art club

Making, slowly. A table. Materials. Music low. Time moving differently.

Art club is the opposite of a loud social. People arrive, pick a seat, start creating. Conversation happens naturally, but making is the centre.

  • Bring nothing, we provide prompts and basic materials
  • Bring something, you can work on your own piece too
  • Occasional micro exhibitions to show what the room has made

The goal is not performance. The goal is presence.
