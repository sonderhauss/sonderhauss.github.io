Workshops

Practical nights. Skill based. Hands moving. People leaving with something real.

Workshops are the most grounded room in the venue. Each session is built around one craft and one outcome. It can be stitch, scent, zines, repair, sound, or design.

  • Small groups and clear facilitation
  • Materials included or clearly listed
  • Documented in a warm, minimal way

This is where Sonderhaus becomes useful, not just beautiful.
