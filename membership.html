Membership

A gentle way to keep the venue alive.

Membership is for people who return. It is not about exclusivity. It is about consistency, early doors, and small perks that make showing up easier.

  • Early invitations and first access to limited tickets
  • Occasional member only moments inside existing rooms
  • Small perks like priority seating or discounts when possible

When you set pricing, keep it simple: one tier that feels fair.
